Top Attractions in Sri Lanka

Sigiriya The Ancient Rock Fortress

Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District near the town of Dambulla in the Central Province.

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic or Sri Dalada Maligawa is a Buddhist temple in Kandy.

Nine Arches Bridge

The Nine Arches Bridge also called the Bridge in the Sky,is a viaduct bridge in Sri Lanka. It is one of the best examples of colonial-era railway construction in the country

Galle Fort

Galle is located in the south of Sri Lanka. A journey to the past sees the remnants of a Dutch past at a World Heritage Site.

Udawalawe National Park

This park is home to several hundred elephants and other rare plants and wildlife.

Bentota Beach

This beach is stunning and spotlessly clean with very few people although sunbeds and refreshments are available here.
